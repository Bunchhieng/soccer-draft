Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

1. Information We Collect

Soccer Pickup Draft is committed to protecting your privacy. Our application:

  • Uses local storage only - all data is stored on your device
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not track user behavior

2. How We Use Information

The only data stored is:

  • Team information (stored locally on your device)
  • Player names (stored locally on your device)
  • Draft preferences (stored locally on your device)

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using browser's localStorage. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (for anonymous usage statistics)
  • GitHub Pages (for hosting)
